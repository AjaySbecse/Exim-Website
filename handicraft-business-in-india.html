Coco peat

Coco peat is the 'coir fiber pith' produced as a bi-product when coconut husks are processed for the extraction of the long fibers from the husk. Coco peat is the binding material that comes from the fiber fraction of the coconut husk. Coco coir/peat is a multi-purpose soil conditioner and growing medium. It is consistent and uniform in texture.
